# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-14

This Privacy Policy explains how the QTI Word Add-in project handles information.

## 1) Data processing model

- The add-in processes document content to generate QTI package output.
- In this open-source implementation, conversion logic runs in the client add-in code.
- The project repository does not include built-in user analytics or telemetry collection.

## 2) Data sent or stored

- Generated QTI packages are created for user download.
- The project does not provide a backend service that stores your assessment content by default.
- If you deploy custom infrastructure, your own hosting and logging settings apply.

## 3) Microsoft 365 and platform providers

Use of Word, Microsoft 365, GitHub, or other platforms is subject to each provider's privacy terms.

## 4) Security

You are responsible for securing your deployment endpoints, access controls, and distribution workflow.

## 5) Your responsibilities

Before distributing to students or staff, confirm your use complies with institutional and legal requirements.

## 6) Policy updates

This policy may be updated over time. The latest version is the one published in this repository.

For support and privacy questions, see [SUPPORT.md](./SUPPORT.md).
